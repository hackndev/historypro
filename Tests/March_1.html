  • Bulgaria – Baba Marta (мартеница)
  • Catholic Church, Feast of Saint Albin
  • Catholic Church, Feast of Saint Monan, Scottish saint
  • Catholic Church, Feast of Saint Swidbert
  • Iceland – Beer Day – This day in 1989 marked the end of beer prohibition
  • South Korea – March 1st Movement Remembrance Day (Samiljeol; 삼일절)
  • Roman Empire – Matronalia in honor of Juno
  • Roman Empire – Feriae Marti in honor of Mars
  • Roman Empire – New Year
  • Roman Empire – The sacred fire of Rome is renewed (See Vesta)
